Guide

  1. How to get started

    Send all sms to the number: +8555120404
    When you register for an account you are automatically opt-in to receive forum feeds
    Note: You can pause to stop receiving feeds at any time by sending the word Pause to the sms number and continue by sending the word Resume to the sms number
    Also: You need at least 1 token to make this request

  2. Join Rooms

    There are 12 engaging rooms to choose from, arranged by categories. You automatically join the Welcome room after registration. Other rooms include:













    You can join a room by sending the keyword: Join Room > (Name of Room) E.g: Join Room > Tech
    Note: You automatically leave a room when you join a new room
    You need at least 1 token to make this request

  3. Discussion and Topics

    Engage in the discussion and share your opinions. You can also update the current topic of a room simply by sending Topic > with the title E.g: Whats trending in Crypto to the sms number
    "Note: " Topic should not be more than 64 characters

    Send the word Discussion to the sms number to see the topic of discussion
    You need at least 1 token to make this request

  4. Sending a Message

    Begin your message with a number, the symbol > and your message to broacast to a specific number of members. Eg: 20 > Hello Everyone will broadcast to only 20 members.

    All message should be 160 characters or less per message.
    Share your opinion and respect the opinions of others, also every message should be moderated.
    To report a user send the word Report, the symbol > and the user's name, E.g: Report > John Doe
    You need at least 1 token to make this request

    Note: Any report made on your account may result in account suspension/deletion.

  5. Tokens and Payment

    Tokens are needed to broadcast messages to everyone on the forum. The number of tokens you have are equivalent to the number members your message would be broadcasted to. Send the word Tokens to the sms number for details on your available tokens.
    Note: You need at least 1 token to make this request
    You can always purchase more tokens for as little as $0.04 per token.

  6. How do delete your account?

    Kindly send us mail with your phone number and registered email to delete account.
    Note: Phone number cannot be used for registration after account deletion
